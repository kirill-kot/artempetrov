Early Years

Early Years December 1, 2013

Artem’s first international ambitions came to realization during the 2013 CIK-FIA Academy Trophy season. This series provide the opportunity for the promising young drivers from all over the world to shine and earn their place in the sport. Artem scored his first international podium at Genk circuit. Since then, he is determined to compete at the top level in any competition he enters. The Academy Trophy karting event contains qualifying session, the elimination heats and 2 main races.

About Artem Petrov

Artem Petrov is a racing driver from Saint-Petersburg, Russia. He finished the 2017 Italian Formula 4 Championship in 5-th position competing for DR Formula team. Artem has accomplished a number of notable results throughout his racing career. Starting from Karting, he made his way to open wheel junior racing series to take part in major competitions like German and Italian Formula 4 championships.
