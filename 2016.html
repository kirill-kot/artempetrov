Formula 4, 2016

2016 Formula 4 Season 2016

Artem made his debut in Italian Formula 4 Championship in 2016 season with DR Formula team. His progress throughout the season resulted 7 podiums in rookie category and 2 more podiums in overall rankings in the second half of the year.

The Russian driver competed against the best junior drivers including Mick Schumacher – the son of the 7-time world champion Michael Schumacher.

Formula 4 traditional event format included 2 qualifying sessions and 3 races.

About Artem Petrov

Artem Petrov is a racing driver from Saint-Petersburg, Russia. He finished the 2017 Italian Formula 4 Championship in 5-th position competing for DR Formula team. Artem has accomplished a number of notable results throughout his racing career. Starting from Karting, he made his way to open wheel junior racing series to take part in major competitions like German and Italian Formula 4 championships.
